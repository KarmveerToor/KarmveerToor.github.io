Target Audience

HP has been working on its Omen line for the past couple of years to serve the high-end gaming community. Now they are taking a swing at more casual gamers with this new HP Pavilion 15. HP says that it’s all meant for people who are interested in gaming, but aren’t necessarily buying their PC solely to play games.
