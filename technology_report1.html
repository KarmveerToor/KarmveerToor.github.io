About The Pavilion Gaming Laptop 15

The new Pavilion Gaming Laptop 15 is, as the model name suggests, a 15.6-inch laptop with a variety of panel options. They include a pair of 1080p options, one with a 144Hz refresh rate and the other with a 60Hz refresh rate, and a 4K display option (60Hz).Pricing starts at $799, which gets you a Core i5 processor, 8GB of RAM, 1TB HDD accelerated with 16GB Intel Optane memory, and a Radeon RX 560X GPU. For those who are willing to spend more, burlier setups will be available, including up to a 6-core chip from Intel, larger HDDs and NVMe SSD options, and a GeForce GTX 1060 with Max-Q.

Specifications

  • CPU:Intel Core i5 or i7
  • RAM:8GB DDR4
  • Storage:1TB HDD accelerated with 16GB Intel Optane memory
  • GPU:Radeon RX 560X, Nvidia GeForce GTX 1050 Ti or GTX 1060 Max-Q GPU
  • Display Options:Include two 1080p options, one with a 144Hz refresh rate and the other with a 60Hz refresh rate, and a 4K display option (60Hz)
